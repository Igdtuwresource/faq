Concept of re-mid term, supplementary exams?

If you have missed minor exams, then in 1/2 weeks, re-mid terms happens. You need to submit medical with application as reason. Your answer sheet will be evaluated from 75% of 30 Marks.
If you have missed end term exam or failed in it, then you need to give supplementray exam with your juniors when they will be having their exam. In both the cases, don't forget to fill a form provided by college for re-appearing.

How marks are calculated for DKM?

60 Marks are for DKM (calls) and 40 are for CSD (events). In CSD, you need to attend the events, attendance is compulsory and from there you will be given marks. In DKM, you need to do particular number of calls, it's duration is fixed and they will give you marks for that.
